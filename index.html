Our Story

Born from a
contradiction.
Built on craft.

Arty Visuals was born in Mt.Elgon from a contradiction: an agriculture student whose eyes were always on the frame, not the field. Ian Kibet carved his own path — driven by an unshakeable passion for photography, film, design and brand identity.

Today, Arty Visuals serves creatives, artists and visionary brands across Kenya who demand work that reflects the same level of ambition they bring to their own craft. Camera to screen, concept to print, idea to identity.

"Arty is not just a name. It is a standard. A promise that what you see was crafted for eyes that refuse to settle for ordinary."

— Ian Kibet, Founder

The Founder

Ian Kibet.
An unconventional
creative.

Ian Kibet studies agriculture — but his true harvest has always been visual art. Driven by an unshakeable passion for photography, film, design and brand identity, Ian founded Arty Visuals to build a studio that stands for something rare: uncompromising quality in everything it touches.

His unconventional journey is the very soul of Arty Visuals. He believes that genuine creativity does not follow a prescribed path — it breaks one.

"Honest art is the translation of existence into expression. It isn't about being the best in the room — it's about being the most authentic to the journey."

— Ian Kibet
